Frequently Asked Questions

How do I upload documents?

Tap the Upload button in Documents and choose your file.

What file types are supported?

PDF, DOCX, and TXT. Images are supported for OCR.

How do I create flashcards?

Upload a document and choose Generate Flashcards, or create manually.

Can I study offline?

Yes. Downloaded materials and flashcards are available offline.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Manage subscriptions through the App Store or Google Play.
